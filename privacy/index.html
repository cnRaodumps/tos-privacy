Privacy Policy

Updated March 11, 2026

Temp Mail stores mailbox settings, active addresses, consent state, and notification preferences on the device so the app can reopen your inbox quickly. Temporary messages are requested from the configured email service only when needed for inbox delivery and are not sold by the app. Crash reporting, analytics, and push messaging stay inactive until the publisher provides production credentials. Abuse, spam, harassment, malware distribution, and unlawful use of disposable addresses are prohibited.
